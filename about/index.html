About Our Leadership

Pastors Steve & Tracey Karst

Pastors Steve and Tracey arrived at The River in February 2023. Steve, originally from Wallace, Idaho, is a passionate visionary with a heart for the people of North Idaho. He longs to lead people into a loving and life-giving relationship with Jesus Christ.

Steve and Tracey have four adopted children — Gabriel, Moriah, Israel, and Ezekiel — and prioritize adoption and foster care ministries. When they're not on mission, you'll find them cheering on the Dallas Cowboys, around a board game, smoking ribs, or dreaming about their next trip to Galaxy's Edge at Disneyland.
