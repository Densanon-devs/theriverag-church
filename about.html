Our Pastors

Pastors Steve & Tracey Karst

Pastors Steve and Tracey arrived at The River in February 2023. Steve, originally from Wallace, Idaho, is a passionate visionary with a heart for the people of North Idaho. He longs to lead people into a loving and life-giving relationship with Jesus Christ.

Steve and Tracey have four adopted children — Gabriel, Moriah, Israel, and Ezekiel — and prioritize adoption and foster care ministries. When they're not on mission, you'll find them cheering on the Dallas Cowboys, around a board game, smoking ribs, or dreaming about their next trip to Galaxy's Edge at Disneyland.

What We Believe

Our Core Beliefs

The River Church is part of the Assemblies of God fellowship. These are the foundational truths that guide our faith and practice.

The One True God

We believe in one God eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

The Deity of Jesus Christ

Jesus Christ is the eternal Son of God, born of a virgin, fully God and fully man.

The Fall of Man

Man was created good and upright but fell through voluntary transgression.

Salvation

Salvation is received through repentance toward God and faith in the shed blood of Jesus Christ.

The Inspiration of Scripture

The Bible is the inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word of God.

Sanctification

Sanctification is the ongoing work of separating from evil and being conformed to the image of Christ.

Baptism in the Holy Spirit

All believers are entitled to and should earnestly seek the baptism in the Holy Spirit.

Divine Healing

Divine healing is provided for in the atonement and is the privilege of all believers.

The Church & Its Mission

The Church is the Body of Christ, called to seek and save the lost.

Ordinances of the Church

We observe two ordinances: water baptism by immersion and the Lord's Supper.

The Blessed Hope

The resurrection of those who have fallen asleep in Christ and the rapture of believers.

The Millennial Reign

The visible return of Christ and His reign on earth for one thousand years.

The Final Judgment

There will be a final judgment of the wicked dead.

The New Heavens & New Earth

We look for new heavens and a new earth wherein dwells righteousness.

The Ministry

A divinely called and scripturally ordained ministry has been provided by the Lord.
